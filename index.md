## research experience

**University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus**, Aurora, CO  
Graduate researcher — Advisor: Daniel Denman, PhD  
2021–present  

Characterizing neural response to electrical brain stimulation in mice  

- high-density electrophysiological recordings of neural response to electrical stimulation with spatiotemporal precision  
- behavioral discrimination of electrical stimulation in primary visual cortex  
- implemented analyses and experimental paradigms in python  

---

**University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus**, Aurora, CO  
Professional Research Assistant — Advisor: Cristin Welle, PhD  
2016–2019  

Investigating vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) to enhance motor learning and plasticity  

- developed chronic VNS implant in mice  
- studied effects of VNS on forelimb reach task learning  
- investigated mechanisms of plasticity using electrophysiology, calcium imaging, and viral tracing  

---

**Colorado State University**, Fort Collins, CO  
Undergraduate researcher — Advisor: John Rash, PhD  
2014–2016  

Studying electrical connections of the nervous system using transmission electron microscopy  

- characterized CNS gap junctions in zebrafish mauthner cells  
- identified Cx29 and Kv1.1 hemichannels linking axons to myelin
